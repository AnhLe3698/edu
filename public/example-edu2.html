Question 1: What is Kinematics?

  • Study of forces
  • Study of motion without considering the forces
  • Study of heat
  • Study of light

Wiki Entries

Kinematics is the branch of classical mechanics which describes the motion of points, bodies (objects), and systems of bodies without considering the forces that cause them to move.
