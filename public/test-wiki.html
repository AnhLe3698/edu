Gross Domestic Product

Also known as: GDP

Type: Economic indicator

Measures: Market value of all goods and services produced within a country in a given period

Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is a measure of economic activity within a country. It's the total market value of all goods and services produced within the borders of a country in a specific time period. More information...

Definition

GDP is the total value of everything produced by all the people and companies in the country. It doesn't matter if they are citizens or foreign-owned companies, if they are located within the country's boundaries, the government counts their production as GDP. More information...

Methods of Calculation

GDP can be calculated using three different approaches: the production approach, the income approach, or the expenditure approach. More information...

Usage and Limitations

GDP is primarily used to provide a snapshot of a country's economic health. However, there are limitations to what GDP can tell us. It does not consider the distribution of wealth within a country or the informal economy. More information...
